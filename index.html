Hello, I am Anand Rai, a PhD student at the Computer Science and Engineering department of the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur under Prof. Animesh Mukherjee . My broad research interests lie in the field of Fairness in Information Retrieval. With the increasing use of technology and algorithms to make important decisions, it is crucial to ensure that these systems do not perpetuate or amplify biases or discrimination towards certain groups. My research focuses on developing fair and equitable information retrieval systems that can benefit society as a whole. Through my work, I hope to contribute to creating a more just and equitable world. Thank you for visiting my profile page and I welcome any opportunities for collaboration or discussion on this important topic.

I received my Masters in Data Science from IIT Hyderabad, where I was co-advised by Maunendra D Desarkar and RamaKrishna Upadrasta . My doctoral research focused on addressing issues concerning the fairness in AI based online pedagogical tools. I completed my bachelors degree in computer science from IIITDM, Jabalpur in 2012. I've also spent time doing research at S&P Capital IQ, AMADA CO. JAPAN and Ministry of Steel. I am a recipient of the UGC Junior Research Fellowship.

I am actively looking for internship opportunities, please reach out if you think I am a good fit.
